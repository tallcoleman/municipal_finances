# Housing Starts

Source: https://data.ontario.ca/dataset/ontario-s-housing-supply-progress

This data shows how many new homes have been started towards municipal housing targets.

Years provided:

- 2023
- 2024

Included:
- municipality
- 10-year housing targets (2022-2031)
- total housing progress since 2022
- target for applicable year
- total applicable year housing progress, comprised of: 
  - housing starts
  - additional residential units
  - long-term care beds
  - postsecondary student beds (new as of 2024)
  - licensed congregate retirement home suites (new as of 2024)
- housing progress expressed as a percentage of the annual target
- housing target status

Examples of housing that is not included:

- postsecondary student beds (2023 only)
- licensed congregate retirement home suites (2023 only)
- group homes
- transitional/supportive housing

Data are reviewed in the year following the reporting year. Data published here reflect post-review adjustments and may not align with Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation data as published in its annual surveys (Starts and Completions survey). Over a two-year period, however, statistics across both organizations will align.
